# Unsupervised Learning Interview Questions & Topics

## Core Concepts
- **Clustering Algorithms:**
  - **K-Means:** Algorithm steps, initialization (K-Means++), choosing K (Elbow method, Silhouette score).
  - **Hierarchical Clustering:** Agglomerative vs Divisive, linkage criteria (single, complete, average, Ward).
  - **DBSCAN:** Density-based clustering, epsilon, min_samples, handling noise/outliers.
- **Dimensionality Reduction:**
  - **Principal Component Analysis (PCA):** Eigenvalues/Eigenvectors, covariance matrix, variance explained.
  - **t-SNE & UMAP:** Non-linear dimensionality reduction, mainly for visualization.
- **Anomaly Detection:** Isolation Forests, One-Class SVM.

## Common Questions
1. How does K-Means differ from Hierarchical Clustering?
2. What are the limitations of PCA? Can it handle non-linear data?
3. How do you evaluate the quality of clusters when there are no true labels?
